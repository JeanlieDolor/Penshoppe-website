About Us

Penshoppe is a leading fashion brand in the Philippines that offers a wide range of clothing and accessories for men, women, and kids. Our products are inspired by the latest fashion trends from around the world and are designed to cater to the needs of the modern-day consumer. Known for its fresh and innovative take on casual wear, PENSHOPPE offers high quality apparel, innerwear, accessories, and fragrances from a unique design perspective for a variety of personalities. An inclusive brand that has an age encompassing relevance and appeal.PENSHOPPE is positioned as the go-to brand for a variety of personalities looking to express themselves through fashion, at an attractive value that complements their lifestyle.

Our History

Penshoppe was established in 1986 and has since become one of the most recognized fashion brands in the Philippines. Over the years, we have expanded our product line to include a wide range of clothing and accessories for men, women, and kids. Today, Penshoppe has over 300 stores in the Philippines and has also expanded to other countries in Southeast Asia.

Our Mission

At Penshoppe, our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality and fashionable clothing and accessories at an affordable price. We believe that fashion should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to make our products available to customers from all walks of life.
